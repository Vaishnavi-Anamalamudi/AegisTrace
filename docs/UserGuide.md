# User Guide

## Sign In

Open `http://localhost:8084` and authenticate with a configured account.

## Dashboard

Use the dashboard to review:

- Security events
- Alerts
- Scan history
- Incidents
- Evidence
- MITRE tactic summaries
- Risk score indicators

## Scans

Run scans only for authorized targets. Review open ports, services, status, duration, and risk score after completion.

## Incidents

Incident reports capture a title, summary, risk score, impact analysis, and remediation steps.

## Evidence

Evidence records preserve evidence ID, source, severity, summary, timestamp, and chain-of-custody context.

## Exports

Use `/api/scans/export.csv` to export scan history for reporting or handoff.
